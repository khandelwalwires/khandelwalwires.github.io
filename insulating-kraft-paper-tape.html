Insulating Kraft Paper Tapes

Insulating Kraft Paper Tapes are obtained by slitting top quality Electrical Grade Insulating Kraft Paper on high-precision slitting machines.

Applications

Insulating Kraft Paper Tapes are used for covering aluminum and copper wires and strips used in oil cooled distribution and power transformers, and in current transformers, CT/PT metering units, capacitors, etc.

Features

Available in widths starting from 4 mm and upwards with inside diameter 50 mm and customer specified outside diameters.

Slit on state of the art high-speed slitting machines using cutters made from high Carbon, high Chromium steel for precise and burr-free slitting.
