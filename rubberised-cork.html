Rubberised Cork Sheets & Components

Rubberised Cork Sheets & Components are produced from high grade cork granules which are imported from the Mediterranean region of Spain and Portugal. These cork granules are then intimately bonded with an Elastomer (Neoprene, Nitrile or SBR). This provides a material in which the natural compressibility of cork is combined with the resilience and mechanical strength of rubber. The cellular construction of cork, together with the inherent properties of rubber, makes Rubberised Cork a perfect gasket material.

Applications

Rubberised Cork Sheets & Components are widely used in Automobile, Transformer, Switchgear and Heavy Engineering Industries.

Features

Rubberised Cork Sheets are available in thickness ranging from 1 mm to 25 mm and sheet size 610 mm x 915 mm.

Rubberised Cork Components are available in the form of washers, strips, and customer specified drawings.

Our Rubberised Cork Sheets, Strips, and Washers retain elasticity for long periods of time under extreme temperature and liquid immersion conditions.

Rubberised Cork is manufactured as per the Indian Standard IS:4253 (Part II), 1980.
