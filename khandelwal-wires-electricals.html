Khandelwal Wires & Electricals Pvt. Ltd.

The manufacturing unit of Khandelwal Wires & Electricals Pvt. Ltd. was set up due to popular demand from our valued customers who indicated that there was a dearth of manufacturers of quality aluminium wires & strips for use in oil cooled transformers in Eastern India. We took up the project to manufacture precision and high quality aluminium wires & strips and the first consignment rolled out in April, 2011.

The quality of our products has so far been appreciated by the leading transformer manufacturers in India and corroborated by repeat orders.

As a result of our dedication to maintaining quality and completing orders well in time, we have already been recognized as reliable manufacturers of DPC Aluminium Wire, DPC Aluminium Strip, Super Enameled Aluminium Wire, Nomex Covered Aluminium Wire, Nomex Covered Aluminium Strip, Bare Aluminium Wire, Bare Aluminium Strip, and other insulated aluminium conductors by our valued clientele.

Our customers’ continuous support and encouragement has driven us to set up a second manufacturing unit in a very short span of time. We have installed state-of-the-art machinery and incorporated cutting-edge technology to ensure that our wires and strips are best-in-class in terms of quality and reliability.
