Transformer Insulation

Insulation is the heart of a transformer.

Of all the raw materials that go into manufacturing a transformer, insulation is one of the most important. It has been found that insulation failure is the leading cause of transformer failure and accounts for more than 25% of all causes. We at Khandelwal Insulations believe in providing our valued customers with the best quality insulating materials at most reasonable prices. We are the leading manufacturers, distributors, and suppliers of Electrical Grade Insulating kraft Paper, Insulating Presspaper, Insulating Pressboard, Insulating Crepe Paper, Epoxy Diamond Dotted Paper, Rubberised Cork and other insulating materials in India.

Transformer Winding

A transformer works by transferring energy through magnetically coupled windings.

The entire transformer is build around its primary and secondary windings.
