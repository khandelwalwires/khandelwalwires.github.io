Khandelwal Insulations Pvt. Ltd.

Khandelwal Insulations Pvt. Ltd. was established in the year 1985. Seeing the business opportunities in the power sector, we started out as wholesellers of insulating materials for use in fans, motors, and transformers. Development of strong partnerships with leading pressboard and specialty paper manufacturers led to our specialization in pulp based insulation for oil cooled transformers. With over four decades of experience in the industry, we have secured our position among the leading manufacturers, distributors, and solution providers of transformer insulation in India.

Apart from leading distribution, power, and instrument transformer manufacturers, our clientele includes state power distribution utilities of Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Orissa, Uttar Pradesh, etc.

We are authorized distributors of Insulating Presspaper, Insulating Pressboard, Pre-Compressed Pressboard, Moulded and Machined Pressboard Components, and other value added products manufactured by Senapathy Whiteley Pvt. Ltd., Epoxy Diamond Dotted Paper manufactured by Senapathy Symons Insulations Pvt. Ltd., and Electrical Grade Insulating Kraft Paper manufactured by ITC Ltd.
